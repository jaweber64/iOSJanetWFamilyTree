FAMILY

The Weber Adventure - A Brief History

Mark and Janet met while at Valparaiso University and eventually began dating. After graduation, they both took their first jobs out of college with Unisys near Philadelphia, PA. Mark finally popped the question (at Valley Forge National Park on a freezing December night)! We moved back to IL to be near family. Mark worked for Pandex which eventually became Baxter. Janet worked for Fermilab and coached gymnastics. We bought our first house, and got married in 1990. Then came the kids!

Christian, Joseph, Aaron, Hannah (move to bigger house), Nathaniel and Luke! After Joseph, Janet stayed home full time. Preschool, elementary school - eventually home school, Sunday school, soccer, swimming, baseball, one season of football, summer bible camp, soccer refereeing, highschool, lifeguarding, swim coaching, college and more! Now, the oldest is "off the Weber payroll" and is out on his own. The next three are in college and the younger two are in highschool. Somewhere in there we moved to our current home in Cary and Mark's career took him to Nanosphere, Inc. then to Fenwal, and finally to Fresenius Kabi.

The empty nest is just around the corner - yikes! Janet is taking some coursework at the local community college, getting ready to find employment or start a business. Mark recently bought a fishing kayak and is spending more time on the water. We both spend time on Zillow dreaming about a retirement that includes a lake house.

And that's about it. All are doing well. Life is very fulfilling and crazy busy!!
